Farming Fields - Account Deletion

This page explains how users of the Farming Fields app can request account and data deletion.

App: Farming Fields
Developer contact: support@farmingfields.app

How to request account deletion

  1. Open the app and log in.
  2. Go to Settings -> Account -> Delete Account.
  3. Confirm your deletion request in the app.

If you cannot access the app, email us from your registered email/phone details:

  • support@farmingfields.app
  • Subject: Delete My Farming Fields Account

What data is deleted

  • Account profile data (name, contact details, role).
  • Farm listing and marketplace data associated with your account.
  • User-generated records linked to your account where deletion is supported by system rules.

What data may be retained

  • Security, fraud-prevention, and legal-compliance logs when required by law.
  • Limited backup records during system retention windows.
Standard retention period after account deletion request: up to 30 days.

Partial data deletion (without deleting account)

At this time, partial deletion requests are not supported as a separate self-serve flow. Users can request full account deletion using the methods above.
