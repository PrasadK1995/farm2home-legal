1. Introduction

Farming Fields ("we," "our," "us," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information when you use our mobile application, website, and related services ("Service").

2. Information We Collect

A. Personal Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, password
  • Profile Information: User role (Farmer/Buyer), profile picture, farm name
  • Farm Information: Farm location, farm area, crops grown, contact details
  • Crop Information: Crop type, quantity, pricing, harvest date, crop start date
  • Communication Data: Messages sent between farmers and buyers
  • Media Files: Photos/videos of crops (optionally uploaded)

B. Information Automatically Collected

  • Location Data: GPS coordinates (with permission)
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, device ID, mobile network info
  • Usage Data: Features accessed, time spent, clicks, interactions
  • System Information: IP address, browser type, crash logs, error reports
  • Firebase Analytics: App performance metrics, user flow data

C. Third-Party Data

  • Weather API data for your location
  • Market price data from agricultural databases
  • Map data from OpenStreetMap

4. Information Sharing

A. What We Do NOT Share

  • ❌ We do NOT sell personal data to third parties
  • ❌ We do NOT share data for marketing to other companies
  • ❌ We do NOT share financial information with unauthorized parties

B. What We DO Share

  • Google Firebase: User authentication, profile data, messages (encrypted)
  • Weather Service Providers: Location coordinates for weather forecasts
  • Amazon AWS: Crop images (with access controls)
  • Legal/Law Enforcement: When required by subpoena or court order

C. User-to-User Sharing

When farmers and buyers connect:

  • Buyer sees farmer's farm location (general area, not exact coordinates)
  • Buyer can call/message farmer using provided contact
  • Farmer can view buyer's purchase requests
  • Both parties can message each other in-app

5. Data Security

🔒 Security Measures:
  • ✅ All data transmitted using HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • ✅ Firebase security rules restrict unauthorized access
  • ✅ Passwords encrypted with Firebase Auth (salted hash)
  • ✅ Authentication tokens expire after inactivity
  • ✅ AWS S3 bucket with encryption and access controls
  • ✅ Regular security monitoring and updates
  • ✅ Firestore backup and recovery systems

⚠️ Disclaimer: While we implement industry-standard security measures, no transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Privacy Rights & Data Control

You Have the Right To:

  • Access: View all personal data collected about you
  • Update: Modify or correct your profile information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-Out: Disable push notifications in Settings
  • Control: Manage location permissions in device settings
  • Revoke: Remove app permissions anytime
  • Port: Export your data in machine-readable format

How to Exercise Your Rights:

  1. Open the app → Settings → Account
  2. View, Edit, or Delete your information
  3. Or email: privacy@farmingfields.app

8. Children's Privacy

Farming Fields is not designed for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete such information and terminate the child's account.

For users aged 13-18, parental consent is recommended before using this app.

9. Location Privacy

  • Access: App requests location permission when needed (first map access)
  • Usage: Used to show nearby farms, market data, and weather
  • Control: Users can disable location in device settings anytime
  • Precision: Farm locations shown as general area, not exact coordinates
  • Sharing: Precise location NOT shared with other users
